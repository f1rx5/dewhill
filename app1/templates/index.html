About Our Project

Wayanad,the green paradise in the God's Own Country is loaded with charming waterfalls,caves, resorts, and homestays. Still the surpassing beauty of Dew Hill stands as an outstanding retreat with its unique blend of comfort & luxury of modern indulgences in the midst of mist-capped mountains and greens meadows. Apart from providing a fantastic view of the mighty Banasura Hills,Dew Hill mirrors the splendor of its clean and pristine environment, offering an enchanting and hypnotizing experience. The views ar truly spectacular, opening to lush valleys of tea plantations,springs & lakes, and the wild forest of Wayanad.
